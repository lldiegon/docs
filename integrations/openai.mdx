[You can get an API key here](https://platform.openai.com/account/api-keys).

## Instructions for getting the API key
Log in or create an account with the link above and click on 'API keys' after you're logged in.

<img
  style={{ borderRadius: '0.5rem' }}
  src="/images/integrations/openai/create-new-key.png"
/>
Click on the green 'Create new secret key' button.
You get a windows where you can give it a name, you can call it 'Wisepim' and click Create Secret Key.

<img
  style={{ borderRadius: '0.5rem' }}
  src="/images/integrations/openai/create-new-key-2.png"
/>

Copy the secret key you've just created.

<ResponseField name="OpenAI Token" type="string" required>
The open AI token starts with 'sk-', copy it to add it to your account.

Example: `sk-abcdef1234567890abcdef1234567890abcdef1234567890`

</ResponseField>


## How to add your API key to WISEPIM

Go to WISEPIM, log in and click on [Settings](https://wisepim.swiftsync.ai/settings) and go to the 'Account' tab. 

Here you can fill in the Open AI token and also choose your prompt.
When you've added it, click on 'Update account' to save the key to your account.


<img
  style={{ borderRadius: '0.5rem' }}
  src="/images/integrations/openai/openai-wisepim.png"
/>

All done! You can now use AI to enrich your products!
